Mindfulness Techniques

Mindfulness helps you stay present and reduces stress. Here are a few techniques to practice daily:

  • Deep Breathing: Focus on your inhale and exhale for 5-10 minutes.
  • Body Scan: Pay attention to sensations from head to toe.
  • Mindful Walking: Walk slowly and notice each step and breath.
  • Gratitude Journaling: Note 3 things you are grateful for each day.
  • Meditation Apps: Use apps like Calm or Headspace for guided sessions.
