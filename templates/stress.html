Stress Management

Stress is a normal part of life, but chronic stress can affect your mental and physical health. Try these simple techniques:

🏃 Exercise regularly to release tension.
🧘‍♂️ Practice deep breathing or meditation daily.
📝 Break tasks into smaller, manageable steps.
👥 Connect with friends or family to share feelings.
🌿 Take short breaks and spend time outdoors.
