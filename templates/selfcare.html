Self-Care Tips

Build small daily habits that improve your mental and emotional well-being.

💧 Drink water and eat nutritious meals.
🌿 Take breaks and spend time outdoors.
🧘 Practice mindfulness or meditation.
🎨 Engage in hobbies you enjoy.
😴 Ensure sufficient sleep and rest.
